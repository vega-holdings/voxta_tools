# What is Voxta?
Voxta is a platform for interacting naturally with an AI powered companion using voice chat or text chat.  
Different AI services and models are available to choose from for text generation, text to speech and speech to text.
Voxta requires services to work.  
You can host those yourself for free or use online, paid services.  
Cost will depend on what online services are chosen.
The best way to understand what Voxta is, is to check out [Voxtaâ€™s YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@Voxta) channel and look at some of the videos!
### Voxta Cloud
For newcomers and beginners, [Voxta Cloud](https://doc.voxta.ai/docs/voxta-cloud) presents an easy-to-install, all-in-one solution. However, as itâ€™s an online service, its use will deplete Voxta credits.
Voxta Cloud is not the only paid online solution available.  
Check out [services](https://doc.voxta.ai/docs/services-overview/) for more.
### Voxta Local
For a free solution ideal for users who have a beefy computer and more advanced knowledge on how to setup AI models, Voxta can be used locally.  
Check out [services](https://doc.voxta.ai/docs/services-overview/) for more.
### Voxta x Virt-a-Mate
Voxta can be used for multiple applications; one of them being to connect Voxta with Virt-a-Mate.  
Virt-a-Mate (aka VAM) is an adult sandbox to create, modify and interact in real time with a 3D simulated model, either on Desktop or in VR.  
Check out [Virt-a-Mate](https://doc.voxta.ai/vam/what-is-virt-a-mate/) for more.
