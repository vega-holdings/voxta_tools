# Scenarios Not Visible

> Check Patreon-Discord sync if scenarios are missing.

## Answer

If scenarios are missing or not visible, ensure that your Voxta Patreon account is successfully synced with your Discord account. Access to Voxta features often requires an active subscription and linked accounts.
