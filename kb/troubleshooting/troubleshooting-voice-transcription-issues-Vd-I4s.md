# Troubleshooting Voice Transcription Issues

> Steps to diagnose voice pickup issues using the Transcription Playground and F2 logs.

## Answer

To diagnose transcription issues:
1. Go to the **Transcription Playground** (also referred to as Speech To Text Playground) in the Voxta interface.
2. Speak and observe if the volume indicator moves to verify microphone input.
3. Press **F2** in Voxta Desktop to open the logs and check for any specific error messages.
