# Diagnosing AI Talking Loops

> Identifies the LLM as the primary cause for talking loops.

## Answer

Issues where the AI gets stuck in a talking loop are typically caused by the **LLM** (Large Language Model). This behavior is usually not related to the TTS (Text-to-Speech) or STT (Speech-to-Text) systems.
