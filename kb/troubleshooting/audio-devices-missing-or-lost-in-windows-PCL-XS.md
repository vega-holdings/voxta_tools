# Audio Devices Missing or Lost in Windows

> Restarting the computer can fix issues where Windows loses audio devices, often triggered by Oculus updates.

## Answer

Windows can sometimes lose track of audio devices, causing them to disappear from selection lists. This issue is frequently observed in conjunction with Oculus updates.

**Solution:**
Restart your computer to refresh the Windows audio subsystem before investigating further or filing a bug report.
