# Troubleshooting Unexpected Credit Depletion

> Check usage history to identify credit drains like ElevenLabs.

## Answer

Check your usage history at [https://portal.voxta.ai/history](https://portal.voxta.ai/history) to see exactly where credits were spent. High-quality TTS services like ElevenLabs are often the cause of rapid credit depletion due to their high cost.
