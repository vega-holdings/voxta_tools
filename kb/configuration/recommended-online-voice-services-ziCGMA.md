# Recommended Online Voice Services

> Voxta Cloud and ElevenLabs are the main supported online voice providers.

## Answer

Currently, **Voxta Cloud** and **ElevenLabs** are the primary recommended options for online voice generation. Other available services are often restricted to SFW content or offer lower quality.
