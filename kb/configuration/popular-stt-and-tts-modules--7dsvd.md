# Popular STT and TTS Modules

> Community recommendations for STT and TTS modules.

## Answer

For Speech-to-Text (STT), **Whisper** is a commonly used module. For Text-to-Speech (TTS), **Coqui** and **F5-TTS** are popular choices among users.
