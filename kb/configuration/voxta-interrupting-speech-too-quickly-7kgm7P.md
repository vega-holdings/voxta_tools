# Voxta Interrupting Speech Too Quickly

> Adjust speech transcription advanced options to fix premature interruptions.

## Answer

To prevent Voxta from interrupting too quickly, navigate to the **Speech Transcription** settings and look for **Advanced Options**. There you can adjust the pause threshold or sensitivity settings.
