# F5-TTS vs Coqui XTTS

> Comparison of F5-TTS and Coqui XTTS as local TTS options.

## Answer

F5-TTS is a supported local, free alternative to Coqui XTTS. Some users prefer F5-TTS over Coqui, though neither typically matches the quality of cloud-based solutions like ElevenLabs.
