# F5-TTS vs XTTS Performance

> F5-TTS is recommended over XTTS for better speed and accuracy.

## Answer

Users report that **F5-TTS** is significantly faster and often more accurate compared to **XTTS**. If you are experiencing slow generation times or accuracy issues with XTTS, switching to F5-TTS is recommended.
