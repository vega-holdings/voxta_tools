# Voxta Cloud Portal

> The Voxta Cloud Portal provides a centralized location for managing API keys, reviewing pricing, and monitoring account credits and usage history.

## Answer

The Voxta Cloud Portal is accessible at [https://portal.voxta.ai/](https://portal.voxta.ai/).

Use this portal to manage your account and services:

*   **API Management:** Retrieve your **Voxta Cloud API Key**.
*   **Billing & Usage:** Check your credit balance, view usage history, and review specific pricing costs for different AI services and features.
*   **Account Details:** Access other general account details and configurations.
