# Purpose of FFmpeg in Voxta

> FFmpeg handles audio format conversion required for STT and TTS.

## Answer

FFmpeg is used to convert audio formats (such as PCM to WAV) within the Voxta pipeline. It is a necessary dependency for utilizing Speech-to-Text (STT) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) services.
