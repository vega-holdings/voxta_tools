# Playgrounds
Voxta provides multiple interactive environments to explore and test AI functionalities:
## Transcription Playground
This is a space where you can test the Speech-to-Text service. You speak, and the platform converts your spoken words into written text using services like Azure Speech Service. Itâ€™s a practical way to see how accurately and quickly the AI can transcribe your speech.
![](https://doc.voxta.ai/images/uploads/snag-0434.jpg)
## Voice Playground
Here, you can experiment with Text-to-Speech capabilities. Type in text and hear the AI bring your words to life using various voices provided by services like Coqui. It allows for testing different accents, intonations, and speech tempos to find the perfect voice for your needs.
![](https://doc.voxta.ai/images/uploads/snag-0435.jpg)
## Text Generation Playground
Allows you to interact with the AI in a conversational manner. You can input dialogue as a system, user, or assistant, and then prompt the AI to generate a response based on the conversationâ€™s context. This tool is useful for simulating exchanges and testing the AIâ€™s ability to produce relevant and coherent text. Itâ€™s a convenient way to see how the AI would handle real-life interactions, complete with performance metrics like speed and token count.
